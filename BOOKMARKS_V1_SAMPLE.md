# Bookmark Mau V1 (de xay dung form Word)

Quy tac dat ten:

- Format: `<role>_<thu_tu>_<truong_nho>`
- Khong dau, khong khoang trang, chi dung `a-z`, `0-9`, `_`
- `thu_tu` bat buoc la so (`1`, `2`, `3`...)
- He thong tu nhan dien role thuoc nhom Nguoi hay Tai san

## Nhom role de xuat

- Nguoi: `chu_dat`, `vo_chong`, `cha`, `me`, `con`, `chau`, `con_dau_re`, `nguoi_nhan`
- Tai san: `thua_dat`, `nha`, `tai_san_khac`

## Ghi chu nghiep vu ngay thang

- Neu nguoi dung nhap `yyyy`, he thong luu noi bo `01/01/yyyy` de tinh toan
- Khi xuat vao Word, neu gia tri goc la nam thi hien `yyyy`
- Neu la ngay day du thi hien `dd/mm/yyyy`
